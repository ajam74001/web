About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the computer science program at the University of Illinois at Chicago, working with professor Abolfazl Asudeh. I received my master's and bachelor's degree from KOC University (Istanbul, Turkey) and AmirKabir University (Tehran, Iran) respectively.

My research areas generally lie in data science and Machine and deep learning. My current research projects mainly focus on time series forecasting and highly rare event prediction.
